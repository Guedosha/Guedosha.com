Little Life Server Rules
  1. You are allowed to swear, but don't use any slurs.
  2. You may not be toxic or harass other players. This includes bringing in-game conflict into real life.
  3. You may not spam the in-game chat.
  4. Texture packs are allowed but you can't use X-Ray or anything that could be considered an unfair advantage.
  5. Allowed clients are vanilla, Optifine, Lunar, and Badlion. Forge and Fabric are unfortunately not allowed
  6. You may not use macros or botting. e.g. Baritone, auto clickers, etc. For Java players, you are allowed to use F3 + T to hold down a key.
  7. You may not intentionally cause the server or players' games to run slower, crash, or be kicked.
  8. Joke NSFW is allowed as long as it isn't realistic or erotic but no gore or shock content is allowed.
  9. You may not discuss real-life politics or religion. Vague religious imagery and references are allowed for the sake of roleplay but discussing or debating real-life religious or political topics is strictly prohibited.
  10. You may not combat log. This includes intentionally causing yourself to lose connection from the server or to be kicked.
  11. You are allowed to break bedrock, end portal frames, and other "permanent" blocks(doing so was originally a bug but became an intended feature.)
  12. You are allowed to use sand(Image) and TNT(Image) dupers which are some more bugs that became intended features
  13. You may not abuse game breaking bugs. You must report them by opening a ticket or messaging Mart on discord
  14. Notes:
    • This server is semi-anarchy which means you can do literally anything you want so long as it doesn't break these rules. This means you can build, grief, kill, back-stab, create governments/alliances, and overthrow governments to your heart's content.
    • Except in very extreme cases, you will almost always be told that you are breaking the rules with no punishment so don't worry about accidentally breaking them.
    • Once again, if you have any questions or don't understand what something means, DM Mart.
